Dipl. Inf. Univ.

Dietmar Fackelmann

Hello, I am a computer scientist since 2001. I studied at the Ludwig-Maximilian University in Munich. Since then i have been interested in IT security and IT networks. I worked for about four years as an IT security employee at an large insurance company. I worked with firewalls, routers and VPNs. Then I worked for five years as a manager for the IT infrastructure of the state of Baden-Württemberg. The work included the tasks of running a data centre with security devices and many remote locations.

Since 2021 my focus has been on Android smartphone forensics

Due to restructuring, I moved to the forensic area in 2015. At first I was afraid that the forensic topics would be very limited. Coming from the wide range of network topics, I was initially unable to recognise the diversity of the topics in the forensic area. I worked in Windows forensics and Linux forensics for five years. Then I had the opportunity to work in network forensics. The rise of smartphones led me to move into smartphone forensics. After three years and a steady increase of topics, I was able to focus on Android smartphones.
